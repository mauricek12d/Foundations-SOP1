Inception

Memento tells the story of Leonard Shelby (Guy Pearce), a man with short-term memory loss who is trying to solve his wife’s murder. Using Polaroid photos and body tattoos to track clues, Leonard’s story unfolds in reverse — revealing just how unreliable memory and motive can be. Directed by Christopher Nolan, this film is a mind-bending exploration of identity, trauma, and truth.
